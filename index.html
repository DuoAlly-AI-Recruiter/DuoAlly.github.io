About DuoAlly AI

DuoAlly AI is pioneering the next generation of AI companions for single-player gaming. Our AI-powered assistant provides players with an interactive, game-aware ally, designed to enhance solo experiences across action, adventure, horror, and puzzle genres. With DuoAlly AI, players are never alone, enjoying real-time support, hints, and companionship tailored to each unique gaming genre.

Features

  • Game-Aware Chatbot: An intelligent assistant that understands in-game events and adapts in real time.
  • Genre-Specific Responses: Supports player needs, from horror game encouragement to puzzle-solving hints.
  • Advanced Learning: Continuously improves with reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) and knowledge graphs for accuracy.
  • Future Expansion: Plans to include voice interaction and animated companions for an immersive experience.

Our Team

DuoAlly AI is developed by a dedicated team of 5, combining expertise in machine learning, gaming, and software development. Together, we strive to redefine single-player gaming by creating AI that feels like a genuine co-player.

Research & Development

Our team recently published a framework evaluating GenAI effectiveness using both objective and subjective metrics. Additionally, our latest publication introduces a novel approach using Neo4j knowledge graphs in retrieval-augmented generation, enhancing chatbot accuracy and context awareness.
